Identifying Structural Changes in UK Power Generation Mix

This app applies K-Means clustering to identify structual changes in UK power generation mix.
The structural breaks are marked by red vertical lines in the charts & the optimal number of clusters is determined by the Davies-Bouldin scores.
For yearly data, due to the smaller sample size, bootstrap is employed to calculate the stability of clustering results.
For detailed analysis, please refer to GitHub.
(data source: NESO).
